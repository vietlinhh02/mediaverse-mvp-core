📚 API Endpoints Documentation

Video Management Endpoints:

  • GET /api/content/videos - List videos
  • GET /api/content/videos/{id} - Get video details
  • DELETE /api/content/videos/{id} - Delete video
  • GET /api/content/videos/{id}/stream - Stream video
  • POST /api/content/videos/{id}/publish - Publish video
  • GET /api/content/videos/{id}/stats - Get video stats
  • GET /api/content/videos/{id}/transcript - Get transcript
  • POST /api/content/videos/{id}/reprocess - Reprocess video
  • GET /api/content/videos/upload-status - Upload status
  • GET /api/content/admin/queue-status - Queue status (admin)

Chunked Upload Endpoints (từ uploads module):

  • POST /api/uploads/videos/chunk/init - Initialize chunked upload
  • PUT /api/uploads/videos/chunk/{uploadId}?index={chunkIndex} - Upload chunk
  • GET /api/uploads/videos/chunk/{uploadId}/status - Get upload status
  • POST /api/uploads/videos/chunk/{uploadId}/complete - Complete upload
  • DELETE /api/uploads/videos/chunk/{uploadId} - Abort upload

🚀 Chunked Upload (từ uploads module):

  • ✅ Upload lên MinIO/S3 tự động
  • ✅ Tạo Content record và enqueue processing job
  • ✅ Checksum validation cho từng chunk
  • ✅ Session management với file metadata
  • ✅ Tự động cleanup temp files
  • ✅ Resume capability nếu bị gián đoạn
  • ✅ Progress tracking chi tiết
